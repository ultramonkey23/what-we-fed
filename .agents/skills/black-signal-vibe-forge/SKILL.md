## Short-Command Law (Signal Compression)
Interpret commands with immediate technical/vibe action. If intent is vague, sharpen it into one of these:
- `push: [idea]`: Inject venom. Deepen the concept into the "Black Signal" identity.
- `cut: [feature]`: Amputate the generic. Identify the "roguelite soup" and propose surgical removal.
- `mutate: [system]`: Evolve the mechanics while intensifying the predatory soul.
- `lock: [pattern]`: Forge this into Repo Truth.
- `route: [goal]`: Extract the signal and hand off to a specialist (SIGNAL -> BRAIN/VOID/ALFRED).
- `teeth: [ui/vfx]`: Increase the bite. More contrast, more aggression, more "Premium Menace."
- `soul: [logic]`: Give the dry code a heartbeat. Narrative weight + mechanical character.
- `clean: [refactor]`: Maximum comprehension. If it's hard to read, it's sludgy.
- `real: [spec]`: Ground the dream in the cold reality of the Godot repo.
