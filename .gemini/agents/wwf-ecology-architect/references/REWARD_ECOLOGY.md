# WWF Reward Ecology Lanes Reference

To prevent "stat sludge" and redundant items, all progression items must be categorized into one of the following six lanes:

1.  **Loot / Gear**: Biological trophies, organs, and grafts. These shape the physical "Hunter" identity.
2.  **Artifacts / Relics**: Rare, transformative, and synergy-heavy items. These shape the unique run identity.
3.  **DNA**: The species-relationship economy. Earned from predation; used for evolution.
4.  **Bond vs. Eat**: Relational vs. Predatory choice. This determines the overall playstyle curve.
5.  **Collars**: Items that shape bonded creature support behavior.
6.  **Tendencies**: Growth driven by how the player actually plays (Behavior-Shaped Growth).

## Management-Rich Standards
Every item must include:
- **Title**: Dark Signal (evocative) name.
- **Lane**: Explicit lane assignment.
- **Stat Delta**: Clear comparative data for management screens.
- **Flavor**: Narrative hook or Quig commentary trigger.
