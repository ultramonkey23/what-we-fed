# Power Fantasy Ashclaw Design Specifications

## Core Philosophy: "Intimidating, Cool, Dominant"

Ashclaw must evolve from "dangerous potential" to "genuine apex predator terror." Every stage should be genuinely intimidating and cool.

## Evolution Stages - "Growing Menace"

### Stage 1: "Deadly Progeny" - 64x64 pixels
**Age**: Newly born, already dangerous
**Personality**: Predatory instinct, no innocence

#### Visual Design
**Body Structure**:
- Compact but muscular build
- Low, predatory stance (ready to pounce)
- Head proportionally large for intimidation
- Tail already developed, whip-like
- Claws visible even at this stage

**Color Scheme**:
- Primary: Charcoal gray (#1A1A1A)
- Secondary: Blood orange (#CD2626)
- Accents: Burning ember core (#FF4500)
- Eyes: Glowing orange-red embers

**Intimidation Features**:
- Eyes burn with predatory intelligence
- Claws already sharp and dangerous
- Mouth shows teeth when open
- Constant low growl energy field
- Movement is predatory, not playful

#### Animation Focus
**Idle**: 6 frames, 1.0s loop
- Frame 1: Coiled predator stance
- Frame 2: Head tilt, scanning for prey
- Frame 3: Subtle claw extension
- Frame 4: Tail twitch, anticipation
- Frame 5: Weight shift, ready to strike
- Frame 6: Return to coiled stance

**Attack**: 4 frames, 0.4s total
- Frame 1: Explosive lunge start
- Frame 2: Mid-air, claws extended
- Frame 3: Impact, ember explosion
- Frame 4: Cool landing, predator pose

**Cool Factor Elements**:
- Every movement looks like hunting
- No cute or playful animations
- Constant predatory focus
- Energy signature shows danger, not curiosity

### Stage 2: "Rising Threat" - 96x96 pixels
**Age**: Adolescence, power growing unstable
**Personality**: Aggressive, testing dominance

#### Visual Design
**Body Structure**:
- Lanky but powerful build
- Overgrown claws (too large, more dangerous)
- Spiked ridge along back
- Tail longer, more whip-like
- Muscles visible under skin

**Color Scheme**:
- Primary: Darker charcoal (#0F0F0F)
- Secondary: Aggressive red (#8B0000)
- Accents: Unstable orange bursts (#FF6B35)
- Eyes: Burning red-orange flames

**Intimidation Features**:
- Eyes constantly burning with aggression
- Claws leave energy trails when moving
- Spiked back shows dangerous evolution
- Unstable energy field crackles around body
- Size already intimidating compared to player

#### Animation Focus
**Idle**: 8 frames, 0.8s loop
- Frame 1: Aggressive stance, claws extended
- Frame 2: Muscle tension, power building
- Frame 3: Head shake, unstable energy
- Frame 4: Tail lash, aggressive movement
- Frame 5: Claw scrape, testing weapons
- Frame 6: Energy burst, power surge
- Frame 7: Weight shift, predatory movement
- Frame 8: Return to aggressive stance

**Attack**: 6 frames, 0.5s total
- Frame 1: Power charge, energy gathers
- Frame 2: Explosive leap, energy trail
- Frame 3: Mid-air spin, claws extended
- Frame 4: Impact explosion, screen shake
- Frame 5: Dominant landing, intimidation pose
- Frame 6: Cool recovery, ready for more

**Cool Factor Elements**:
- Movements are aggressive and unpredictable
- Energy effects show growing power
- Every pose looks threatening
- Attacks feel genuinely dangerous

### Stage 3: "Apex Dominator" - 128x128 pixels
**Age**: Full maturity, ultimate predator
**Personality**: Confident dominance, terrifying presence

#### Visual Design
**Body Structure**:
- Massive, muscular build
- Perfectly developed killing claws
- Crown of spikes on head
- Whip-like tail with blade tip
- Armor-like skin plates

**Color Scheme**:
- Primary: Near-black (#0A0A0A)
- Secondary: Blood red (#8B0000)
- Accents: Dominant ember core (#FF4500)
- Eyes: Burning red-orange inferno

**Intimidation Features**:
- Eyes burn with intelligent malevolence
- Claws glow with constant energy
- Spiked crown shows royal dominance
- Energy field melts nearby environment
- Size is genuinely terrifying

#### Animation Focus
**Idle**: 10 frames, 0.6s loop
- Frame 1: Apex predator stance, dominance
- Frame 2: Slow head turn, surveying territory
- Frame 3: Claw extension, weapon display
- Frame 4: Tail blade test, deadly movement
- Frame 5: Muscle flex, power display
- Frame 6: Energy pulse, environmental effect
- Frame 7: Chest expansion, dominance breath
- Frame 8: Spine ridge glow, power surge
- Frame 9: Weight shift, confident movement
- Frame 10: Return to apex stance

**Attack**: 8 frames, 0.4s total
- Frame 1: Power concentration, energy sphere
- Frame 2: Explosive launch, earth shatters
- Frame 3: Mid-air spin, death from above
- Frame 4: Impact, screen-filling explosion
- Frame 5: Dominance roar, energy wave
- Frame 6: Cool landing, earth cracks
- Frame 7: Intimidation pose, victory display
- Frame 8: Ready stance, eternal threat

**Cool Factor Elements**:
- Every movement shows absolute confidence
- Energy effects are overwhelming
- Poses are genuinely intimidating
- Attacks feel world-ending

## Combat Support Behavior - "Cool Coordination"

### Support Strike System
**Principle**: Ashclaw attacks should look cool and coordinated with player

#### Visual Coordination
**Signal Sequence**:
1. **Eye Contact**: Ashclaw locks eyes with player (1 frame)
2. **Energy Sync**: Both characters' energy fields pulse together (2 frames)
3. **Nod of Understanding**: Cool, confident coordination (1 frame)
4. **Simultaneous Strike**: Both attack together, energy trails connect (3 frames)
5. **Impact Synchronization**: Explosions occur simultaneously (1 frame)
6. **Victory Pose**: Both strike cool poses together (2 frames)

#### Power Evolution Effects
**Baby Form Support**:
- Quick ember strikes, 50% player damage
- Cool coordination, but still learning
- Energy signature: small but precise

**Teen Form Support**:
- Coordinated ember bursts, 75% player damage
- Unstable but powerful coordination
- Energy signature: wild but effective

**Adult Form Support**:
- Devastating ember explosions, 100% player damage
- Perfect, deadly coordination
- Energy signature: overwhelming, screen-filling

### Special Support Abilities

#### "Ember Storm" - Area Denial
**Visual**: Ashclaw creates storm of ember projectiles
**Cool Factor**: Screen fills with burning embers
**Coordination**: Player benefits from chaos

#### "Claw Dance" - Multi-Strike
**Visual**: Rapid claw attacks with energy trails
**Cool Factor**: Too fast to follow, deadly beautiful
**Coordination**: Player attacks between claw strikes

#### "Inferno Roar" - Power Boost
**Visual**: Ashclaw roars, empowering both characters
**Cool Factor**: Screen shakes, energy explosion
**Coordination**: Both characters gain temporary power boost
