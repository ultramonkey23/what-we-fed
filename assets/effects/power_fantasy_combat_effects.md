# Power Fantasy Combat Effects Specifications

## Core Philosophy: "Spectacular, Screen-Dominating, Overwhelming"

Every combat effect should dominate the screen and scream "I AM POWERFUL." Subtlety is the enemy of cool factor.

## Screen-Dominating Impact System

### Attack Impact Hierarchy - "Visual Power Progression"

#### Light Attack - "Sharp Execution"
**Duration**: 0.3 seconds total
**Screen Impact**: 30% screen coverage
**Particle Count**: 50-70 particles
**Philosophy**: Fast, clean, stylish precision

**Visual Sequence**:
1. **Wind-up Flash** (0.05s):
   - Sharp white flash at impact point
   - Energy gathers in tight sphere
   - Screen subtly brightens
   - Sound: Sharp crack

2. **Impact Explosion** (0.1s):
   - Directional energy burst (cone shape)
   - 40-50 sharp white energy particles
   - Screen shake: Light, quick vibration
   - Impact flash: Bright blue-white

3. **Cool Follow-through** (0.15s):
   - Fading cyan energy trails
   - 10-20 lingering spark particles
   - Screen slowly returns to normal

**Cool Factor Elements**:

- Stylish energy trails

### Performance Optimization
**Particle Budgeting**:
- **Normal Combat**: 100-200 particles max
- **Heavy Combat**: 300-500 particles max
- **Ultimate Effects**: 800+ particles (acceptable for special moments)
- **Environmental Effects**: 50-100 particles constant

**LOD System**:
- **High Quality**: Full particle count, all effects
- **Medium Quality**: 70% particles, reduced screen effects
- **Low Quality**: 50% particles, essential effects only

### Animation Timing Standards
- **Quick Actions**: 60fps, sharp timing
- **Heavy Actions**: 60fps with dramatic pauses
- **Ultimate Actions**: 60fps with slow-motion moments
- **Environmental Effects**: 30fps acceptable
