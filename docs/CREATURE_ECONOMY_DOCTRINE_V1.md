# WHAT WE FED — CREATURE ECONOMY DOCTRINE V2.1 (The Siralim Upgrade)

## 1. THE TRAIT SYSTEM (Siralim-style Synergy)
Each creature lineage in the **Living Codex** now possesses a unique **System-Lit Trait**.

- **Extraction**: When a creature is "Eaten," its Trait is extracted and stored in the Codex.
- **Splicing**: Between levels (Management-Rich), the player can splice these Traits into their own form or their Bonded creatures.
- **Synergy**: Traits are categorized by the sonic genre of the creature's origin (e.g., a "Funk Trait" and a "Prog Metal Trait"). Combining them creates "System Breaches"—overpowered, rule-breaking synergies.

## 2. UPDATED LANE BREAKDOWN
- **Lane 1 — Initial Bond Unlock**: Sequence stabilization.
- **Lane 2 — Predation Debt**: System friction.
- **Lane 3 — Bond Rank**: Identity evolution (Milestones).
- **Lane 4 — Creature Level**: Performance tuning.
- **Lane 5 — Trait Depth (NEW)**: Mastery of extracted traits and synergy potential.
- **Lane 6 — Lineage DNA**: Currency for splicing and evolution.
- **Lane 7 — Rare Specimen Hunt**: High-purity signal variants.

## 3. THE KAIJU ASCENSION (Godzilla Scale)
- Progression now leads toward **Ascension Milestones**.
- At max Bond Rank and Trait Depth, a creature can undergo "Ascension," drastically increasing its visual scale and transforming the combat field into a "Godzilla-scale" apex encounter (macro-pressure).

## 4. ANTI-DRIFT RULES
- Do not let Traits become "Stat Sludge." Each Trait must have a unique, mechanical effect.
- Trait Splicing is a "Management-Rich" activity. Never during combat.
- The tone of splicing must remain "Kinetic Surrealism"— anatomical rewriting, not just menu toggles.
