## Correction Intent

- Preserve hard lane clarity between Bond Rank and Creature Level.
- Stop progression overlap before it propagates into UI, implementation, and balance.
- Keep creature evolution meaning bound to relationship milestones, not routine stat tracks.

## Active Corrections

1) Correction ID: CANON-CORR-001
- Title: Bond Rank vs Creature Level Split Lock
- Status: ACTIVE
- Source File: `docs/BOND_RANK_VS_CREATURE_LEVEL_SPLIT_V1.md`
- Rule:
  - Bond Rank is post-unlock, rare, milestone-based, and primary owner of meaningful evolution.
  - Creature Level is post-unlock, regular, practical, and never owner of major identity metamorphosis.
  - First unlock is its own one-time event and is not Bond Rank.

2) Correction ID: CANON-CORR-002
- Title: Reward Flow Timing (End-of-Level Burst)
- Status: ACTIVE
- Source File: `docs/REWARD_FLOW_CORRECTION_V1.md`
- Rule:
  - Major deliberation (Bond/Eat fate, Artifact drafting) is deferred to end-of-level payoff bursts.
  - Combat resolution is restricted to immediate stat surges and non-deliberative growth.
  - Premium rewards scale with level performance (S/A/B/C/D rank).

3) Correction ID: CANON-CORR-003
- Title: Living Codex + Entry/Return Identity Lock
- Status: ACTIVE
- Source File: `docs/LIVING_CODEX_DOCTRINE_V1.md`
- Rule:
  - The player is the real-world person admitted through the "Entry" interface.
  - The Living Codex is a biomechanical DNA archive re-instantiated after field collapse.
  - The Lair is the interface wound/reconstitution site.
  - "Respawns" and "Saves" are retired in favor of "Re-instantiation" and "Recall."
  - Native life perceives the player as an "Admitted Outsider" and "Thief of Lineage."

## System Enforcement Clauses

- Any feature proposal that grants major identity evolution through Creature Level is invalid.
- Any feature proposal that makes Bond Rank a frequent +stat treadmill is invalid.
- Any UI proposal that collapses Bond and Level under one generic growth label is invalid.
- Any economy proposal that rewards both lanes with the same outcome class is invalid.
- Any design that requires mid-combat deliberation or pause-state reward drafting is a canon violation.
- Any narrative or UI element that treats the player as a native "Chosen One" or "Hero" is a canon violation.
