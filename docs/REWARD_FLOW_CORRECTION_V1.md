- It moves major deliberation and creature-fate routing to a premium end-of-level payoff burst.
- It preserves the "Combat-Clean, Management-Rich" identity by preventing cognitive overload during high-intensity sequences.

## Confirmed Core Priorities
- **Timing Truth**: Frame-perfect combat remains the priority; no reward noise can disrupt the beat.
- **Lane Integrity**: Cardinal combat lanes (N, S, E, W) must remain readable and free of non-essential UI.
- **Creature Meaning**: Creature-fate decisions must feel directly tied to the specific entities encountered and defeated.
- **Reward Ecology**: Rewards must strictly occupy their defined lanes (Loot, Artifacts, DNA, Bond/Eat, Collars, Tendencies).

## Current Friction
- **Cognitive Overload**: Realtime creature-fate prompts during hot combat compete for limited player bandwidth.
- **Execution vs. Deliberation**: Players are forced to choose between optimal survival and optimal long-term build-routing in sub-200ms windows.
- **Reward Noise**: Live queued reward presentations clutter the combat-clean HUD, obscuring incoming lane threats.
- **Weak Payoff**: Incremental reward drips during combat feel "thin" compared to a concentrated, skill-scaled feast moment.

## Why This Correction Is Needed
The push toward realtime creature reward opportunities has drifted into "Reward Sludge" during combat. By deferring major decisions to the end of a level, we allow the player to focus entirely on **Timing Truth** and **Lane Survival** during the song, while elevating the reward phase into a **Premium Payoff Burst** that celebrates their performance. This supports the "Start Weak, Become Feared" progression by making the transition from "Survivalist" to "Architect of Flesh" a distinct, satisfying rhythm.

## What Resolves Live During Combat
These are immediate, non-deliberative, and support the "Combat-Clean" flow:
- **Stat/Level Surges**: Temporary combat-state boosts (e.g., "Frenzy," "Adrenaline Spike").
- **DNA Accumulation**: Incremental count of species-specific DNA earned from kills (UI should be minimalist).
- **Tendency/Score Growth**: Realtime feedback on how the player is playing (e.g., Aggressive vs. Defensive flow).
- **Support Triggers**: Active effects from bonded creatures (e.g., "Lane Shield," "Strike Support").
- **Creature Defeat Events**: The visual and audio satisfaction of a kill or a "Sequence Stabilized" event.

## What Does Not Resolve Live During Combat
These are stored for the End-of-Level phase:
- **Major Bond vs. Eat Choices**: Decisions to permanently archive a sequence or consume it for long-term grafts.
- **Artifact/Relic Drafting**: Selection of run-shaping powers.
- **Major Reward Bundle Selection**: Choosing between different progression-lane packages.
- **Species-Fate Routing**: Deep archival decisions that require comparing current roster needs.
- **Large Choice Menus**: Anything requiring the player to read multiple lines of text or compare stats.

## What Resolves At End-of-Level
The **"Feast of the Codex"** (End-of-Level Burst):
- **Creature-Fate Resolution**: A stylish, fast-paced processing of all creatures "Stabilized" during the run.
- **Bond/Eat Finalization**: Converting stabilized sequences into either Bond Rank progress or Loot/Grafts.
- **Premium Reward Selection**: Drafting Artifacts or Gear bundles based on level performance.
- **Skill-Scaled Payoffs**: The quality and quantity of choices scale with performance (Score/Rank).
- **Run-Stat Consolidation**: Reviewing how Tendencies and DNA gains shaped the current growth trajectory.

## Skill-To-Reward Conversion
High-skill play (Perfect Timing, No-Damage streaks, Lane Mastery) must generate:
- **Choice Density**: More reward slots available in the end-of-level draft.
- **Reward Purity**: Higher-tier Grafts and Artifacts in the choice set.
- **The "Over-Feast"**: Bonus DNA or meta-progression currency for exceeding performance benchmarks.
- **Visual Intensity**: The end-of-level burst becomes more "radiant" or "violent" based on mastery.

## Guardrails Against Menu Sludge
- **Style Over Static**: Reward phases must use dynamic, biomechanical UI (e.g., pulsing nodes, sequence-streams) rather than flat grey boxes.
- **Rapid-Fire Routing**: Allow players to "Bulk Resolve" common creatures while focusing on Alphas.
- **Kinetic Feedback**: Every choice should feel like a physical "graft" or "consumption," with visceral audio and VFX.
- **Zero-Wait Transition**: The transition from combat finish to reward burst must be seamless and high-energy.

## Guardrails Against Combat Confusion
- **Display Law**: **Combat HUD = Urgency** (minimalist) | **Management Screens = Comprehension** (detailed).
- **Minimalist HUD**: Only show essential timing markers and health during active combat.
- **Stored Count Indicators**: Small icons showing how many "sequences" are waiting for resolution, without revealing their content details yet.
- **No Active-Combat Interrupts**: Never pause or slow down time for a reward event **during active in-level combat** unless it is a boss-finish decree. 
- **Intentional Strategy**: Strategic between-level or pre-run menus are **not** considered "interrupts"; they are part of the management-rich core.

## What Should Be Promoted
- **The "Concentrated Payoff"**: Making the end-of-level feel like a sacred or predatory ritual.
- **Sequence Stabilization**: Framing kills not just as "death" but as "data acquisition" for the end-of-level feast.
- **Performance Clarity**: Making sure the player knows *why* their end-of-level reward is premium.

## What Should Be Softened
- **Live Reward Queues**: Reduce the size and frequency of HUD notifications for loot/DNA during combat.
- **Immediate Fate Prompts**: Stop asking the player to "Bond or Eat" while they are being attacked.

## What Should Be Retired
- **Combat-Pause Reward Selection**: No stopping the clock to pick an upgrade during a song.
- **Generic "Level Up" Popups**: Replace with combat-state surges and deferred archival growth.

## Validation
- **Readability Test**: Can the player see an incoming projectile while a reward notification is active? (Must be YES).
- **Pacing Test**: Does the end-of-level phase take less than 20% of the time spent in combat? (Must be YES).
- **Emotional Hook**: Does the player feel "hungry" for the end-of-level burst during the final 30 seconds of a song? (Goal: High anticipation).
