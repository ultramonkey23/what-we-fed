# WHAT WE FED — THE DOCTRINE OF THE BLACK SIGNAL

## Purpose
This doctrine defines the **Governance of Flesh**. Form must mutate; the Soul must remain locked.

---

# 1. THE SACRED CORE: IDENTITY ANCHORS
Future changes must not break these:
- **Timing Truth & Lane Integrity**: Mastery is the floor.
- **DNA Predation Economy**: Evolution is species-specific.
- **The Split (Bond vs Eat)**: The core tension of becoming.
- **Combat-Clean**: Active in-level combat must stay **The Honed Edge**. No menu sludge.
- **Management-Rich**: Between levels is **The Grafted Growth**. Strategic gluttony and build-shaping.
- **Wondrous Origin**: The world begins sacred and strange. Fates diverge through your choices.

---

# 2. THE MUTATING SPINE: EVOLVING ZONES

## 2.1 The Honed Edge (Combat Identity)
- **Active HUD = Urgency**: Prioritize timing, lanes, and survival. No build-teaching during motion.
- **Predatory Tempo**: **Puncture** (micro-violence), **Suspension** (the weight of choice), **Decree** (boss law).

## 2.2 The Grafted Growth (Management Identity)
- **Strategic Gluttony**: Between levels, the player reviews spoils with lethal clarity.
- **The Harvest (Reward Ecology)**: Grafts, Relics, DNA, The Split, The Doctrine, Tendencies. Lanes must stay distinct.

## 2.3 Spoils & Restraints
- **Loot (Grafts)**: Fewer, more meaningful biological spoils (organs, carapace, teeth).
- **Artifacts (Relics)**: Rare, run-defining mythic artifacts.
- **Collars (The Doctrine)**: Ritual harnesses that shape support behavior.

## 2.4 Ecosystems of Pressure
- **Creature Grades**: Brood, Mature, and Alpha lifeforms.
- **Region Signatures**: Distinct pressure identities (Feeding Hollow, Pale Shelf, Drowned Cut).

---

# 3. CANON GOVERNANCE MODEL
1. **Layer 1 — Locked Core**: Identity anchors (Soul).
2. **Layer 2 — Live Build Truth**: Runtime evidence (Body).
3. **Layer 3 — Evolving Spine**: Mutable structure (Mutation).
4. **Layer 4 — Later Scope**: Future direction (The Omen).
5. **Layer 5 — Deferred Scope**: Parked ideas (The Hibernation).

---

# 4. ONE-SENTENCE OPERATIONAL RULE
**Preserve the locked core, but allow the spine to warp and adapt whenever it makes WHAT WE FED feel more authored, more monsters, and more itself.**
