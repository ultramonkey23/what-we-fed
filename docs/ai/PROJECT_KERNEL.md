## 1. The Skill-Flow Matrix (The "Pulse" of Progression)
To maintain "Timing Honesty" while scaling "Management-Rich" power, we use the Skill-Flow Matrix:
- **Song Complexity (The Floor)**: Increases note density, BPM, and lane-switch frequency. (Player Skill).
- **Character Mitigation (The Ceiling)**: Grafts and Artifacts allow "Mitigation" (e.g., turning a "Miss" into a "Weak Hit" or gaining "Parry Shields"). (Character Power).
- **The Tension**: As complexity rises, the player MUST use management systems to increase their mitigation ceiling.
